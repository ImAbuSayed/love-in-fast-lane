Raat di haneri vich chamke tera roop,
Main labhda haan tainu out of the group.
Kanch warge tere nain shining so bright,
Main karda haan baatan deep in the night.
Zulfan di chhanvich I'm losing my way,
Main rukda ni piche whatever they say.
Bullan te hassa tera pulling me near,
Main banda haan aashiq having no fear.
