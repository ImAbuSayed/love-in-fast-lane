Dilaan di khed vich rolling the dice,
Main jitda haan baazi paying the price.
Zehar wargi tu mehak driving me mad,
Main jarda haan nakhre giving all I had.
Vairiyaan di bheed vich I never care,
Main karda haan wada I will be there.
Kach di eh duniya vich hiding my face,
Main rukda ni baby setting the pace.
