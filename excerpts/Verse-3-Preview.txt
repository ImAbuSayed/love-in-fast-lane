Dil de hanere 'ch labhda main light,
Main larda haan khud naal every single night.
Tuttiyaan umeedan vich breaking the glass,
Main karda haan koshish letting it pass.
Par tera chehra ni fading away,
Main rukda haan othe where you would stay.
Zakhma te marham ni working no more,
Main darda haan kalla knocking your door.
